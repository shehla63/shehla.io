YHP MEDICO is a Health care Android Application. The Application is mainly comprised of 30-40 Screens. MEDICO App has 3 or 4 aspects.
1. Admin - who can register Hospitals, Doctors, Pharmacies, laboratories and their Information.
2.User - who has plenty of services like have an Appointment of Doctors, consult with Doctors, and can send Notification or request for blood donation and can also donate blood to the needy one. Also has number of features to use.
3. Doctor - who can schedule his/her Appointment and manage his/her profile.
The services we have used in this Application are Real time Firebase Database Integration, Push Notification , Chat Box , APIs , Firebase Authentication , Email and Facebook login , OTP service, Payment Gateway using APIs and many more.....
